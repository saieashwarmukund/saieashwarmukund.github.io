About me

I was born in San Jose and was raised in Irvine, California. In high school, I pursued my interests in music and art, as well as come across a new passion, journalism. I was a part of a nationally-acclaimed high school newspaper and, at point, the front-page editor. California has always been home to me so, after graduating from Irvine High School in 2016, I decided to attend UC San Diego for my undergraduate education. It was a home away from home, as the UCSD community welcomed me with open arms of new friends and kind professors. I entered as a Human Biology major, as my mind was set on the medical path. It took me a year realize my true passion lies in creativity and freedom of coding, after taking a Data Science class. I was introduced to Python and have only broadened my horizons of computer proficiency and languages since then.
